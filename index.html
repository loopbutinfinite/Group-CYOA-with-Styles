The Whispering Woods

Introduction

The rain is a relentless, cold drumbeat against the windshield of your beat-up sedan. It's 2:17am and you're hopelessly lost on a gravel road that does not appear on any map. The reasoning for you being out here is due to a frantic call you received moments before, from a distressed family membe. However, you now realize that the situation is even more dire as you notice that your phone is now drained of all its battery and the GPS in your car no longer updates but you see that the last location displayed is Blackwood Forest.

Through a brief break in the heavy downpour of rain, your headlights catch a flicker of light coming from a house that you did not realize was up ahead. You see that the house is a two-story Victorian style home. Standing defiant and crooked as it leans against, the dense and tall, Blackwood Forest that surrounds the house. A single, dim lantern hangs on the porch of the porch of the home. YOu start to contemplate if you should make your way to the home as you see that the alternative is spending the night in your cold car, that is on the verge of potentially sliding down a muddy embankment.
